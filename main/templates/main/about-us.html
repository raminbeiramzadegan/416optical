Our Story

Founded in 2005, 416 Optical emerged from a vision to revolutionize the eye care experience in Toronto. What began as a boutique optical store has evolved into a beacon of excellence in vision care.

Our journey has been defined by an unwavering commitment to combining cutting-edge technology with personalized care, creating an experience that goes beyond traditional optometry.

Today, we stand as a testament to our founding principles: exceptional service, technical excellence, and a genuine commitment to enhancing our clients' quality of life through better vision.
